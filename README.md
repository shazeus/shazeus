## About Me

I build clients, tools, and small projects around the things I actually use.

My background is Minecraft anarchy, CPVP, base hunting, client setups, and utility clients. I am not an old 2b2t player; I come from the wider anarchy scene. Some of my older videos are about anarchy servers, montages, client setups, Klux, YapSavun, and similar Minecraft content.

I develop [Flex Client](https://github.com/shazeus/flexclient) together with my friend ByBurak. We are both owners of the project. Flex is a 1.21.1 combat + utility client for anarchy, and it is the project I care about the most right now.
